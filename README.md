## 👨‍💻 About Me

I’m a **Computer Science student at LUMS** and a **Data Scientist / AI Research Engineer** who enjoys building intelligent systems end-to-end — from **data pipelines, forecasting models, and machine learning workflows** to **LLM systems, research prototypes, agentic automation, and full-stack product deployments**.

My work sits across two strong sides:

- **AI Research Engineering** — LLM optimization, knowledge distillation, LoRA/QLoRA, RAG, agentic AI, applied generative AI  
- **Data Science** — predictive modeling, EDA, forecasting, clustering, survey analytics, ML evaluation, business-facing insights  
- **Full-Stack Development** — React, Node.js, Django, APIs, dashboards, deployment workflows, research-backed software systems

## 📌 What I’ve been working on

- Built an **AQI forecasting system** with real-time ingestion, feature engineering, model benchmarking, and full-stack deployment  
- Prototyped **autonomous AI agent workflows** for scheduling, email triage, and lead generation  
- Worked on **Flash-aware LoRA**, **DGQA**, and **knowledge distillation pipelines** for efficient LLM deployment  
- Built **SensAI**, a clinical documentation prototype with streaming ASR and EMR auto-fill workflows  
- Developed ML systems for **crop recommendation**, **Airbnb investment analysis**, **career readiness**, and **economic inequality research**
